About

Ósk Norðfjörð is an Icelandic abstract artist based in Reykjavík. Her work explores ethereal light, organic textures, and atmospheric moods drawn from Iceland’s landscapes.

Working primarily in acrylic and mixed media, she builds layered, tactile compositions that feel both grounded and luminous—inviting slow looking and quiet reflection. Guided by movement, weather, and memory, her paintings translate the rhythm of lava fields, coastline haze, and winter light into balanced, contemplative forms.
